POOL RULES

Back to Main Page

  • Striker/Midfielder:
    • 2 points for goal
    • 1 point for assist
  • Defender:
    • 3 points for goal
    • 1 point for assist
    • 1 point for clean sheet
  • Keeper:
    • 1 point for win
    • 1 point for clean sheet


4 players maximum from each country.

If there is an injury, one of your subs (of same position) can replace them. None of the previous points that your sub has accumulated will count. There will be no subs for players from eliminated teams.

Any player you choose must be put in the position that they are listed as under this player listings link on the home page.
